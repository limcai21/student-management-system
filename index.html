The Institute of Technical Education (ITE) in Singapore was established on 1 April 1992 as a post - secondary institution. An education from ITE provides graduates with good job and career advancement opportunities as well as opportunities to go for further education and training. Adopting a 'One ITE System, Three Colleges' Education Model, ITE is ready to provide exciting, high - quality, relevant and market - responsive learning for the global economy. ITE offers a wide range of courses to school leaves and working adults, with courses from the School of Applied & Health Science, Business & Services, Design & Media, Electronics & Info - Comm Technology, Engineering and Hospitality.
